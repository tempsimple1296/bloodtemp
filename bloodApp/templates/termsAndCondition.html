Who is a donor?

According to Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (India), a donor means a person who voluntarily donates blood after he has been declared fit after a medical examination, for donating blood, on fulfilling the criteria given here inafter, without accepting in return any consideration in cash or kind from any source, but does not include a professional or a paid donor.
