Message From Developer

In India we loose 3 precious lives everyday,which would have been saved had there been an adequate and well maintained blood stock available for their deed.India has an alarming blood deficit of 35% ,i.e. per 100 patients we have 35 unfortunates who will loose their lives to the lack of availability of blood.The primary reason for this deficit lies with the lack of information and a scepticism regarding the process and distribution.With this project we wish to cover this gap in terms of information and introduce some transparency, where people and the NGO can see the amount of deficiencies and the kind of deficiencies in a particular region and can organise a camp where they will find the maximum number of volunteers with the required blood group to eliminate the same.Its a humble little contribution in order to save a life,which can be ours,yours or a loved one's.

So the Developer would like that Every Indian Must Register to meet the needs of blood.
